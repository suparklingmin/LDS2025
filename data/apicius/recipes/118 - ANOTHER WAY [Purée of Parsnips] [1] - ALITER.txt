MASH THE PARSNIPS, [add] CUMIN, RUE, STOCK, A LITTLE CONDENSED WINE, OIL, GREEN CORIANDER [and] LEEKS AND SERVE; GOES WELL WITH SALT PORK [2].
-----
[1] Again faulty punctuation obscures the text. Carefully compare the following: Tac. and Tor. _Spondylos teres, cuminum_, etc. Hum., List. and G.-V. _S. teres cuminum_, i.e. crush the cumin. Sch. _S. tores_--dry, parch!
[2] _Inferes pro salso_--serve with salt pork or bacon, or, instead of--_Salsum_--salt pork. Dann. Well seasoned with salt! Sch. _infares pro salsa_. For further confirmation of _salsum_ cf. {Rx} Nos. 148-152.
