COOK THE BEANS [2]; MEANWHILE CRUSH PEPPER, LOVAGE, CUMIN, GREEN CORIANDER, MOISTENED WITH BROTH AND WINE, AND ADD [more] BROTH TO TASTE, PUT INTO THE SAUCE PAN [with the beans] ADDING OIL; HEAT ON A SLOW FIRE AND SERVE.
-----
[1] Tor. _Concicla_--_conchis_--_conchicula_--young, immature beans, string or wax, boiled in the shell or pod.
[2] _conchiclam cum faba_--young string beans and (dry, white or kidney) beans, cooked separately of course and mixed when done, ready for service.
