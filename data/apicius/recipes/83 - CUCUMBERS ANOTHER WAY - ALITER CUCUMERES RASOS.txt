[Peeled cucumbers are] STEWED WITH BOILED BRAINS, CUMIN AND A LITTLE HONEY. ADD SOME CELERY SEED, STOCK AND OIL, BIND THE GRAVY WITH EGGS [1] SPRINKLE WITH PEPPER AND SERVE.
-----
[1] Tor. _bis obligabis_--tie twice--for which there is no reason, except in case the sauce should curdle. List. _oleo elixabis_--fry in oil--obviously wrong, as the materials for this stew are already cooked. Sch. _ovis obligabis_--bind with eggs--which is the thing to do in this case.
